PeptidAI Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 06, 2026

Introduction

PeptidAI ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication credentials.

Wellness Data: We collect wellness goals, sleep quality responses, energy level responses, journal entries, preferences, calculation inputs, and scheduling preferences that you voluntarily enter. This data is stored locally on your device and in your private cloud account.

Apple Health Data: On supported iPhone devices, and only with your permission, PeptidAI may access Sleep Analysis and Active Energy Burned from Apple Health to help prepopulate journal entries. Apple Health data is not shared with third-party AI services.

No Personal Data Sold: We do not sell your personal data.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve the PeptidAI service, including performing calculations, maintaining schedules, syncing your private account, prepopulating journal entries when you enable Apple Health, and generating personalized wellness plans when you explicitly consent to external AI data sharing.

Third-Party AI Services and Data Sharing

PeptidAI uses external AI services to generate personalized wellness plans only after you provide explicit in-app consent. Before any data is sent to an external AI service, the app discloses the recipient, the data categories, and the purpose of processing.

Primary AI provider: Google's Gemini API, operated by Google LLC.

Backup AI providers: Groq, Inc. and Cerebras Systems Inc. may be used only if the primary provider is unavailable.

Data sent for AI processing: wellness goals, sleep quality responses, energy level responses, and general health preferences from onboarding. Apple Health data is not sent to external AI services.

Purpose: to generate AI-powered wellness recommendations and a personalized wellness plan.

You may decline AI data sharing, in which case no personal wellness data is sent to external AI services for plan generation. You may also revoke AI consent from Settings.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase with encryption in transit and at rest. Calculation data is stored locally on your device and synced to your private account. AI requests are processed to generate your wellness plan and are not sold by PeptidAI.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase (authentication and data storage)
  • RevenueCat (subscription management)
  • Google Gemini API, operated by Google LLC (AI-powered wellness plan generation with consent)
  • Groq, Inc. (backup AI-powered plan generation with consent)
  • Cerebras Systems Inc. (backup AI-powered plan generation with consent)
  • Apple App Store (subscription billing)
  • Apple HealthKit (optional health data access on supported devices)

Each third-party provider maintains its own privacy and security practices and is expected to provide protection for user data consistent with applicable law and industry standards.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time through the app's Settings page. Upon deletion, all your data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days. You may revoke AI data sharing consent from Settings, and you may revoke Apple Health access in the iOS Settings app.

Children's Privacy

PeptidAI is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy within the app.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@peptidai.com
